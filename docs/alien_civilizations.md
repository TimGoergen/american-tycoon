# Alien Civilizations & Currencies Index

This document lists 100 distinct alien civilizations, their home planets, and their currencies. It is structured into 10 thematic categories representing various sci-fi, fantasy, and cosmic archetypes. 

In the context of an incremental idle clicker game, these races can make contact with Earth sequentially or in groups, unlocking vast new markets that scale Earth's economy by orders of magnitude (e.g., transitioning from Trillions of Earth Dollars to Centillions of alien energetic assets).
